Escape to Paradise

Shangri La Paradise is more than a beach resort—it’s a serene escape to a world of natural beauty and refined luxury. Here, the days drift by with the rhythm of the waves, the scent of tropical blooms fills the air, and every detail is crafted for your comfort. your paradise..

At Shangri La, time slows down. Spend your days floating in our infinity pool overlooking the horizon or being pampered in our world-class spa with treatments inspired by island botanicals. Every moment is designed to soothe your senses.

Dining here is an experience in itself. From fresh seafood caught daily to gourmet international cuisine, our chefs bring flavor and flair to every plate. Whether you’re dining under the stars or sipping cocktails by the pool, every bite is a celebration.

When it’s time to rest, retreat to your luxurious accommodation.Each room thoughtfully appointed with comfort, style, and breathtaking views. Whether it's an ocean-facing balcony or a suite that opens directly onto the beach, you’ll find a space that feels like your own piece of paradise.
